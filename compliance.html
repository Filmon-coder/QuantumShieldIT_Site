What “ready” means

Documented

Policies, procedures, SSP, and POA&M written in clear language—mapped to controls and owned by real people.

Implemented

Accounts, MFA, backups, logging, encryption, vulnerability management, and incident response runbooks in place.

Evidence‑backed

Screenshots, configs, exports, and tickets organized by control so auditors can verify in minutes, not days.

Readiness checklist

CMMC L1/L2

  • Scope & data flow confirmed (FCI/CUI)
  • SSP drafted; POA&M with owners & dates
  • Access control, MFA, logging, backups verified
  • Vendor / MSP agreements reviewed

NIST 800‑171 Mapping

  • 110 controls mapped to policy & evidence
  • Gap analysis with risk ratings
  • SIEM/log retention & vulnerability scans
  • IR plan + tabletop exercise

RMF Artifacts

  • System categorization & boundary diagram
  • Control tailoring & implementation details
  • Contingency plan & test results
  • Continuous monitoring strategy

ISO 27001

  • ISMS scope & Statement of Applicability
  • Risk assessment & treatment plan
  • Internal audit & management review
  • Annex A controls implemented & evidenced

HIPAA

  • Security Rule risk analysis & safeguards
  • BAAs in place; minimum necessary enforced
  • Audit logging & breach notification workflow
  • Annual training & sanction policy

Our 4‑week fast‑track process

Week 1 — Scope & Gaps

Workshop, asset inventory, boundary diagram, initial gap report with risk‑ranked actions.

Week 2 — Policies & Controls

Draft policies/procedures, deploy critical controls (MFA, backups, logging, patching).

Week 3 — Evidence & Training

Collect screenshots/configs, build tickets, run a short tabletop; staff training.

Week 4 — Audit Ready

Finalize SSP/POA&M, close high‑risk gaps, organize evidence binder, plan continuous monitoring.
